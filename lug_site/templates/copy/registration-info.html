Registration Info

  • Registration is restricted to members of the LUG. If you have shown up to more than one meeting (or you hang out in our IRC channel literally all the time), congratulations, you're a member!
  • Before your account is activated, the LUG officers will check to make sure that (a) we know who you are, and (b) you are not a bot.
  • Once you have registered, we'll give you your username. It is autogenerated from your nickname by lowercasing and removing any characters Django doesn't like. (So Sha`Bren becomes shabren.)
