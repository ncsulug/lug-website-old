Rules

This site exists to benefit the NC State Linux community and people who hang out with us. We want to be a friendly, welcoming community that doesn't get shut down by the University. As such, keep these things in mind:

  • Don't be a jerk (especially not to other members).
  • Don't use excessive profanity. (That's what LOUDBOT's for.)
  • Don't post or link to anything not safe for work (or school).
  • Don't try to break the site.
  • Don't intentionally break any laws or University policy.
